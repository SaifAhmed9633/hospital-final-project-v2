Optomology

Ophthalmology at Al-Kasr Al-Aini in Egypt is a leading service dedicated to eye health and vision care. As part of Cairo University's distinguished medical institution, the ophthalmology department offers advanced diagnostic and treatment options for various eye conditions, including cataracts, glaucoma, and retinal disorders. Equipped with modern technology and staffed by expert ophthalmologists, it provides comprehensive care, from routine eye exams to complex surgeries. Renowned for its excellence in patient care and medical training, Al-Kasr Al-Aini's ophthalmology services play a vital role in improving vision and enhancing the quality of life for patients across Egypt.
