Admision&In patient services

The Admission and In-Patient Services at Al-Kasr Al-Aini in Egypt are a cornerstone of its renowned healthcare offerings. Part of Cairo University's historic medical facility, these services provide comprehensive care for patients requiring hospitalization. The hospital is equipped with advanced medical technology, specialized wards, and intensive care units, ensuring high-quality treatment across various medical disciplines. With a dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and support staff, Al-Kasr Al-Aini ensures compassionate and professional care, serving as a vital institution for Egypt's healthcare and medical education.
