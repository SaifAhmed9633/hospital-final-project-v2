OrthopedicSurgery

Orthopedic Surgery at Al-Kasr Al-Aini in Egypt is a premier service specializing in the treatment of musculoskeletal conditions. Part of Cairo University's esteemed medical complex, the orthopedic department is renowned for its expertise in managing fractures, joint disorders, spinal injuries, and sports-related injuries. Equipped with advanced surgical technology and led by highly skilled surgeons, it offers a range of procedures, including joint replacements, arthroscopy, and trauma surgery. With a focus on patient care, rehabilitation, and medical education, Al-Kasr Al-Aini continues to be a leader in orthopedic healthcare in Egypt.
