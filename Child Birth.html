labor

The Childbirth Services at Al-Kasr Al-Aini in Egypt are among the most trusted and comprehensive in the region. As part of Cairo University's prestigious medical complex, the maternity wards are equipped with modern facilities to support both natural and cesarean deliveries. The center offers prenatal care, skilled obstetricians, and neonatal services to ensure the safety and well-being of both mother and baby. With a reputation for excellence and affordability, Al-Kasr Al-Aini plays a pivotal role in providing compassionate and professional maternity care to families across Egypt.
