Plastic surgery

Plastic Surgery at Al-Kasr Al-Aini in Egypt is a leading service offering reconstructive and cosmetic procedures to enhance function and aesthetics. As part of Cairo University's renowned medical complex, the department is staffed by expert surgeons specializing in procedures such as burn reconstruction, cleft lip and palate repair, facial reconstruction, and cosmetic enhancements. Equipped with modern facilities and cutting-edge technology, it provides personalized care to achieve optimal outcomes. Al-Kasr Al-Aini's plastic surgery services are recognized for their commitment to excellence, innovation, and patient satisfaction, serving as a vital resource for individuals across Egypt.
