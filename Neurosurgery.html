Neuro surgery

Neurosurgery at Al-Kasr Al-Aini in Egypt is a center of excellence for the diagnosis and treatment of complex neurological conditions. As part of Cairo University's renowned medical institution, the neurosurgery department provides cutting-edge care for brain, spine, and peripheral nerve disorders. With state-of-the-art surgical facilities and a team of expert neurosurgeons, it offers advanced procedures such as tumor removal, spinal surgeries, and treatment for traumatic brain injuries. Committed to innovation, patient care, and medical training, Al-Kasr Al-Aini remains a leading provider of neurosurgical services in Egypt.
