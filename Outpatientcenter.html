Out patient center

The Outpatient Center of Al-Kasr Al-Aini in Egypt is a prominent healthcare facility affiliated with Cairo University. Renowned for its exceptional medical services, the center offers a wide range of outpatient consultations across various specialties, including internal medicine, pediatrics, orthopedics, and cardiology. Equipped with modern diagnostic tools and staffed by highly skilled physicians and specialists, the center serves as a hub for affordable and accessible healthcare. Its location within Al-Kasr Al-Aini Hospital makes it a critical component of one of Egypt's oldest and most respected medical institutions.
