CardiothoraticSurgery

Cardiothoracic Surgery at Al-Kasr Al-Aini in Egypt is a highly specialized service dedicated to the diagnosis and surgical treatment of heart and chest conditions. As part of Cairo University's renowned medical facility, the department provides advanced care for conditions such as congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, valve disorders, and lung diseases. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by expert surgeons and multidisciplinary teams, it offers procedures ranging from minimally invasive surgeries to complex cardiac and thoracic operations. Al-Kasr Al-Aini is a leader in cardiothoracic care, combining clinical excellence with medical education and research.
