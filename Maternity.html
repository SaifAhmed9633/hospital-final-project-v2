Al Kasr Al Eini Maternity

At Al Kasr Al Eini Hospital, we understand that pregnancy and childbirth are unique, life-changing experiences. Our Maternity Care Program is designed to provide you with compassionate, personalized, and expert care every step of the way—from conception to delivery and beyond. Our experienced medical team is here to ensure you feel supported, informed, and confident in your journey toward parenthood. Comprehensive Prenatal Care From your first visit to the hospital to the birth of your baby, our prenatal care services are designed to support your health and the health of your growing baby. Our team of obstetricians, midwives, and nurses will guide you through every stage of your pregnancy, offering: Regular check-ups and screenings to monitor the health of both mother and baby. Ultrasounds and diagnostic tests to track development and address any concerns early. Prenatal classes to help you prepare for childbirth, newborn care, and breastfeeding. Personalized care plans that take into account your individual health needs and preferences. Labor & Delivery When the big day arrives, you can trust that you and your baby will be in caring and experienced hands. Our labor and delivery suites are equipped with state-of-the-art technology, providing a safe, comfortable environment for childbirth. Our team of obstetricians, anesthesiologists, and nursing staff will support you in various birthing options, including: Natural childbirth with minimal interventions. Epidural or pain relief options to make your delivery experience as comfortable as possible. Cesarean sections when necessary for the safety of mother and baby. Specialized care for high-risk pregnancies. We also offer rooming-in options, allowing your newborn to stay in your room for immediate bonding and breastfeeding. Postpartum Care The care you receive doesn’t stop after delivery. We believe in providing ongoing support to ensure a smooth transition for both mother and baby. Our postpartum care includes: Follow-up appointments to monitor your recovery and address any concerns. Lactation consultants available to assist with breastfeeding challenges and techniques. Newborn care support, including guidance on sleep routines, diapering, and immunizations. Mental health resources, including counseling and support groups for new mothers. Neonatal Care Our Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) provides the highest level of care for newborns who need specialized attention, including preterm infants or those with health conditions at birth. Our skilled neonatologists and neonatal nurses are committed to the wellbeing of every baby in our care. Family-Centered Care At Al Kasr Al Eini Hospital, we recognize that the birth of a baby is a family event. We encourage your family members to be actively involved in the experience. We offer: Family-friendly rooms designed to accommodate partners and support people during labor and recovery. Support for partners through educational resources, counseling, and relaxation techniques. Visitation policies that allow for flexibility and family inclusion while maintaining a restful and healing environment.
